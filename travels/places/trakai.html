Trakai

Trakai is a popular tourist destination, and for good reason. Trakai Castle is one of Lithuania’s most iconic landmarks. Visitors can take a boat trip to the castle and explore its many towers and chambers. Trakai is also a great place to try Lithuanian cuisine, such as kibinai, a traditional Lithuanian pastry.
