Bergen auf Rügen

Bergen auf Rügen is a small town in the heart of Rügen. The Ernst-Moritz-Arndt tower offers stunning views of the town and the surrounding countryside. It was built in 1863 and is named after Ernst Moritz Arndt, a German writer and politician. St. Marienkirche was built in the 13th century and is known for its beautiful stained glass windows. The former cloister,Benedikt-Haus,is now a museum and houses exhibitions on the history of Bergen auf Rügen and the surrounding region. Kids (and adults) might prefer the toboggan ride.
