Nowy Dwór

Town 50 kilometers north of Warsaw that’s most well known for the Modlin fortress - one of the largest 19th-century fortresses in Poland. It’s located on the Narew river. It was originally constructed by the French from 1806 to 1812 to fortify the border the Russian Empire.
