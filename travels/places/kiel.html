Kiel

One of Germany’s major maritime centres, known for a variety of international sailing events, including the annual Kiel Week, which is the biggest sailing event in the world. Kiel is also known for the Kiel Mutiny, when sailors refused to board their vessels in protest against Germany’s further participation in World War I, resulting in the abdication of the Kaiser and the formation of the Weimar Republic.
