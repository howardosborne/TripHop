Namur

The capital of Wallonia and a strategic location at the confluence of two rivers, Namur is a charming and relaxed city that offers a lot of history and nature. You can visit the imposing Citadel, the elegant Saint Aubin’s Cathedral, the cosy Old Town, and the scenic Meuse valley.
