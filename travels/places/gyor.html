Gyor

This elegant city in north-western Hungary is a major industrial and cultural hub. It has a beautiful old town with baroque and neoclassical buildings, as well as modern attractions such as the Audi Hungaria factory and museum. You can see the Cathedral, the Town Hall, and the Rába Quelle Thermal Spa.
