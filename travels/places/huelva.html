Huelva

Huelva is a port city in southwestern Spain, at the mouth of the Odiel and Tinto rivers. North of the center is whitewashed Santuario de Nuestra Señora de la Cinta chapel, dating from the 15th century. Huelva Museum hosts an extensive archaeological collection. A statue of explorer Christopher Columbus overlooks the palm-flanked square of Plaza de las Monjas.
