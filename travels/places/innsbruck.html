Innsbruck

The capital of Tyrol, Innsbruck is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and winter sports lovers. You can ski or snowboard on the slopes of the Nordkette mountain range, or hike and bike in the summer. You can also visit the Golden Roof, the landmark of Innsbruck that was built by Emperor Maximilian I, or enjoy the cultural attractions of the city, such as the Hofburg palace or the Tyrolean Folk Art Museum.
