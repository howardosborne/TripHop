Bansko

Bansko is a popular ski resort in the Pirin Mountains that attracts winter sports enthusiasts from all over Europe. It has modern facilities, diverse slopes, and a lively après-ski scene. Bansko also has a charming old town with traditional stone houses, taverns, and museums.
