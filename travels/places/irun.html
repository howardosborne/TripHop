Border town just over the border from Hendaye on the French side, It’s a practical place which is usually passed through by tourists on their way to the adjoining resort of Hondarribia or San Sebastian. Nevertheless, there are things to see and do including watching the annual Alarde (military review) commemorating vitory over the French in 1522.
