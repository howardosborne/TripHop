Munich

The capital of Bavaria is a popular destination for its Oktoberfest, its beer halls and its fairy-tale castles. You can enjoy the festive atmosphere at Marienplatz, see the famous Glockenspiel clock, visit the impressive Munich Residenz palace and museum, and take a day trip to Neuschwanstein Castle, one of the most spectacular castles in the world
