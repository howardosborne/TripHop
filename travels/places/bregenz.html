Bregenz

The capital of Vorarlberg, Bregenz is a scenic city on the shores of Lake Constance. You can enjoy the lake views and activities, such as swimming, boating, or cycling along the shore. You can also visit the Bregenz Festival, which features opera performances on a floating stage on the lake every summer. Bregenz is also close to some of the most beautiful alpine regions in Austria, such as the Bregenzerwald or the Arlberg.
