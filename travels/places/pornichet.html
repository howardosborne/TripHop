Pornichet

Pornichet is pretty town coastal town on the Côte d’Amour sharing a bay with Le Pouliguen and La Baule. The resort is more or less a contination of La Baule, but is quieter. If you tire of the beaches there are miles of well maintained walks and cycle trails in the surrounding countryside.
