Oslo

Oslo is a vibrant city with something to offer everyone. Visitors can learn about Norway’s Viking history at the Viking Ship Museum, admire the sculptures in the Vigeland Sculpture Park, or take a walk around the Oslofjord. Oslo is also a great place to enjoy Norwegian cuisine and culture.
