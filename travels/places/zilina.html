Zilina

Žilina is a beautiful city with a lot to offer visitors. The historic center is home to many well-preserved buildings, including the Budatín Castle, a castle that dates back to the 13th century. Visitors can also visit the Rajecké Teplice spa town, which is known for its thermal springs and spa treatments. Žilina is also a great place to enjoy Slovak cuisine and culture.
