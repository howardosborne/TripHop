Olsztyn

Ranked highly in quality of life, income, employment and safety, it’s been noted as one of the happiest places to live in Poland. Sights of the city include the medieval Old Town and the St. James Pro-cathedral (former St. James Parish Church), which dates back more than 600 years. The market square is part of the European Route of Brick Gothic and the pro-cathedral is regarded as one of the greatest monuments of Gothic architecture in Poland.
