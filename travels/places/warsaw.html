Warsaw

Warsaw is a vibrant city with a lot to offer visitors. The Royal Castle is a must-see for anyone interested in Polish history. Visitors can also visit the Palace of Culture and Science, a towering Art Deco building, and Łazienki Park, a beautiful park with gardens, lakes, and palaces.
