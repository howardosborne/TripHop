Celle

Celle is a north German city filled with half-timbered houses like the ornate Hoppener Haus. Schloss Celle, a grand castle combining Renaissance and baroque elements, is home to a museum, a chapel and a theatre. The Bomann Museum has cultural history exhibits, including a recreated farmhouse. In a colorfully illuminated building, the Celle Art Museum with Robert Simon Collection presents contemporary art.
