Varna

Varna is the largest city on the Bulgarian Black Sea coast and a popular summer resort. It has a long sandy beach, a vibrant promenade, and a lively cultural scene. Varna also has some interesting historical and natural attractions, such as the Roman Baths, the Sea Garden, and the Stone Forest.
