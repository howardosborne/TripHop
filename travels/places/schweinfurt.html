Schweinfurt

City in Lower Franconia that’s a home to the Museum Georg Schäfer which focuses on German art. Another popular place is the Residence, a baroque palace built in the 17th and 18th centuries. It served as the residence of the princes of Hohenlohe-Waldenburg-Schillingsfürst. Today, the palace houses the Schweinfurt Municipal Collections of Art and History.
