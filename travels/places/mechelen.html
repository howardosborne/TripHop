Mechelen

A hidden gem between Brussels and Antwerp, Mechelen is a small but beautiful city that boasts a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant atmosphere. You can visit the magnificent St. Rumbold’s Cathedral, the impressive Palace of Margaret of Austria, the fun Toy Museum, and the cosy Grote Markt.
