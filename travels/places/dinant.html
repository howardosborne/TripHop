Dinant

A picturesque town on the banks of the Meuse river, Dinant is a perfect destination for a day trip from Brussels or Namur. You can admire the stunning Collegiate Church of Notre Dame, the imposing Citadel, the whimsical Saxophone Bridge, and the mysterious Grotto of Dinant.
