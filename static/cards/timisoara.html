Timisoara

The largest city in western Romania, Timisoara is known as the "Little Vienna" for its elegant buildings and squares that reflect its Austro-Hungarian heritage. Timisoara is also a modern and dynamic city that was the first in Europe to have electric street lighting and trams. You can enjoy the cultural and nightlife options in Timisoara, or visit some of its attractions, such as the Orthodox Cathedral, the Huniade Castle, and the Botanical Garden.
