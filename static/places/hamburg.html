Hamburg

The city of Hamburg is Germany's second-largest city and its biggest port. You can experience the maritime flair of this cosmopolitan city by visiting the Speicherstadt warehouse district, the Miniatur Wunderland model railway museum, the Elbphilharmonie concert hall and the Fish Market. You can also enjoy the nightlife and entertainment options at the Reeperbahn district, also known as "the sinful mile".
