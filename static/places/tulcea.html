Tulcea

The Danube Delta is one of the largest and most biodiverse wetlands in Europe, where the Danube River flows into the Black Sea. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a paradise for nature lovers and birdwatchers. You can take a boat tour or rent a kayak to admire the scenery and wildlife of this unique ecosystem. You can also visit some of the traditional fishing villages and sample some of the local cuisine based on fresh fish.
