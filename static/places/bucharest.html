Bucharest

It’s the capital and the largest city of Romania, as well as a dynamic and cosmopolitan metropolis. It offers a contrast between its historical and cultural heritage and its modern and eclectic vibe. You can visit the Palace of Parliament, one of the largest and most extravagant buildings in the world, built by the former communist dictator Nicolae Ceaușescu. You can also explore the old town, where you can find churches, museums, restaurants, and bars.
