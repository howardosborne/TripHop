Bled

Bled is a beautiful town located on Lake Bled, a picturesque lake with a small island in the middle. Visitors can take a boat trip to the island and visit the Bled Island Church, a charming church located on the island. They can also visit Bled Castle, a castle that dates back to the 11th century. Bled is also a great place to try the famous Bled cake, a custard cream cake topped with whipped cream.
