Salzburg

The birthplace of Mozart and the setting of The Sound of Music, Salzburg is a charming city with a baroque flair. You can explore the historic Old Town, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, or visit the Hohensalzburg Fortress, which offers panoramic views of the city and the surrounding mountains. You can also take a day trip to the nearby Salzkammergut region, where you can enjoy the crystal-clear lakes and picturesque villages.
